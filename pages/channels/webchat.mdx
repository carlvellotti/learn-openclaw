# WebChat: Your Testing Ground

WebChat is built into OpenClaw. There is nothing to install, nothing to configure, and nothing to break. It runs on localhost as part of the gateway.

### When to Use It

- **First-time setup** — Verify your agent works before connecting external channels
- **Testing skills and prompts** — Iterate on SOUL.md changes without spamming your real channels
- **Quick access** — When you are at your computer and want to talk to your agent without switching apps
- **Debugging** — When a channel is misbehaving, test the same message in WebChat to isolate whether the issue is the channel or the agent

### Access

Once OpenClaw is running, open your browser to:

```
http://localhost:3000
```

WebChat does not support group conversations. It is a single-user, single-session interface.
